<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>Overview</h2>

This project presents an interactive Excel-based Sales Dashboard for a fast fashion retail business (Viva Calif), designed to monitor key business metrics and uncover actionable insights. The dashboard provides a consolidated view of sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and operational efficiency using dynamic visuals and slicers.

---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-problem"></a>Business Problem</h2>

The business required a centralized reporting solution to:
- Track overall sales performance and key KPIs in real time
- Understand customer purchasing behavior across channels
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Analyze delivery efficiency and customer satisfaction
- Enable quick, data-driven decision-making without manual reporting

---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="dataset"></a>Dataset</h2>

The dataset consists of transactional sales data for a 3-month period, including:
- Order details (Order ID, Order Date, Ship Date)
- Product categories (T-shirts, Jeans, Tank Tops, etc.)
- Customer attributes (Gender, Order Mode)
- Sales metrics (Quantity, Revenue, Ratings)
- Delivery data (Days to Deliver)
- Location data (State/County - California-based operations)

---

<h2><a class="anchor" id="tools--technologies"></a>Tools & Technologies</h2>

- Microsoft Excel
   - Pivot Tables for aggregation and analysis
   - Slicers for interactivity (Order Mode, Gender)
   - Charts:
      - Line chart (Trend analysis)
      - Bar chart (Product performance)
      - Donut chart (Customer segmentation)
      - Heatmap (Purchase behavior)
      - Map chart (Geographical analysis)
      - Conditional formatting for visual insights

<h2><a class="anchor" id="data-cleaning--preparation"></a>Data Cleaning & Preparation</h2>

- Created calculated fields:
   - Days to Deliver = Ship Date - Order Date
   - Week Number for trend analysis
   - Standardized Gender values (Male, Female, Other, Unknown)
- Structured raw data into tabular format for pivot analysis
- Handled missing and inconsistent values
- Prepared data for dynamic filtering using slicers

<h2><a class="anchor" id="research-questions--key-findings"></a>Research Questions & Key Findings</h2>

1. **Overall Performance**: 2,400 orders, 11,997 units sold, generating $649K revenue with an average rating of 4.0 and delivery time of 2-3 days.
2. **Sales Trend**: Weekly revenue ranges between $40K–$70K, with noticeable peaks around mid-period, indicating consistent demand with periodic spikes.
3. **Customer Behavior**: Majority of purchases come through App and Website, with higher contribution from female customers.
4. **Order Size**: Most customers purchase 1–3 items per order, showing typical retail buying patterns.
5. **Product Performance**: T-shirts and Jeans are the top-selling categories, driving the majority of sales.
6. **Geographical Insights**: Sales are concentrated across California counties, with some regions contributing significantly more.
7. **Delivery Efficiency**: Most orders are delivered within 2–3 days, indicating strong operational performance.
8. **Customer Satisfaction**: Ratings are consistently high (4–5 range), reflecting positive customer experience.

<h2><a class="anchor" id="dashboard"></a>Dashboard</h2>

The dashboard shows:

- Order size distribution
- Product performance with gender-wise breakdown
- Geographical distribution of customers
- Delivery time analysis
- Customer satisfaction trends


---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="final-recommendations"></a>Final Recommendations</h2>

- Focus on top-performing products (T-shirts, Jeans) to maximize revenue
- Strengthen App and Website channels as primary sales drivers
- Introduce strategies to increase average order size (bundles, discounts)
- Target high-performing regions and expand in low-performing areas
- Maintain fast delivery timelines to sustain customer satisfaction
- Leverage high customer ratings for marketing and brand positioning
